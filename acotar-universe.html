A Court of Thorns and Roses

Sarah J. Maas's epic fantasy romance set in the magical land of Prythian — where faerie courts, ancient powers, and fierce love collide.

A Court of Thorns and Roses (ACOTAR) follows Feyre Archeron, a mortal huntress who kills a faerie wolf and is dragged into the magical realm of Prythian as punishment. What begins as a Beauty and the Beast retelling evolves into a sweeping saga of war, politics, trauma, healing, and love across seven powerful faerie courts. The series has sold over 13 million copies worldwide and has been optioned by Hulu for a TV adaptation. With 5 books published and 3 more on the way, the ACOTAR universe continues to expand.
