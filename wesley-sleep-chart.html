💤 Wesley's Sleep Power-Ups

🧸
Pick a Bedtime Buddy
Choose one special stuffed animal or blanket to be your sleep sidekick every night. Holding something cozy helps your body know it's time to rest.
🫁
Balloon Breaths
Pretend you're blowing up a big balloon — breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 counts, then blow out through your mouth for 6. Do it 5 times and feel your body get heavy and sleepy!
⛄
Be a Melting Snowman
Squeeze every muscle in your body really tight — fists, arms, legs, face — hold for 5 seconds, then let everything go limp like you're a snowman melting in the sun. Start from your toes and work up!
🎬
Play a Movie in Your Mind
Close your eyes and imagine your favorite place — a beach, a spaceship, a treehouse. Picture every little detail: the colors, the sounds, how it feels. Your brain will drift right into a dream!
🌊
The Warm Wave
Imagine a warm, golden wave starting at your toes and slowly moving up to your head. As it passes each body part, that part falls asleep. By the time it reaches the top — you're out!
📖
Same Routine, Every Night
Your brain loves patterns! Try doing the same things every night before bed — brush teeth, get in PJs, read a bit, then lights out. After a while your brain will start feeling sleepy automatically.
