How It Works

This collection contains 50 hand-crafted haiku written in Lang Belta, the creole language of the Asteroid Belt. Each poem uses authentic Belter vocabulary and explores themes of Belt life — scarcity, solidarity, freedom, love, labor, and the vast indifference of space. The English translations are poetic rather than literal, capturing the spirit and emotion of each piece. Click the button to cycle through all 50 — no repeats until you have seen them all.
