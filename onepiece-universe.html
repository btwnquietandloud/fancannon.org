⚓ What is One Piece?

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 22, 1997. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate whose body gained rubber-like properties after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy explores the Grand Line with his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, in search of the legendary treasure known as the One Piece to become the King of the Pirates. With over 500 million copies in circulation, it holds the Guinness World Record as the best-selling manga series by a single author.
